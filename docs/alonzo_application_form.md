# Alonzo — Application Form (Google Forms draft)

Copy this straight into **forms.google.com**. Each item lists the **question**, the **field type** to pick in Google Forms, whether it's **required**, and options where relevant. Setup takes ~10 minutes.

> **Tip:** In the form's **Settings → Responses**, turn on "Collect email addresses" and link responses to a Google Sheet (Responses tab → green Sheets icon) so applications auto-collect in one place.

---

## Form title
**Alonzo Mentorship — Application**

## Form description
> Alonzo is a mentorship program for Korean computer science students who want to build their careers in the United States. Fill out this short application and we'll match you with a mentor. It's free. We'll only use your information to run the program and will never share it publicly.

---

## Section 1 — About you

**1. Full name** *(Short answer · required)*

**2. Email address** *(Short answer · required)* — or use "Collect email addresses" in Settings instead.

**3. Preferred language** *(Multiple choice · required)*
- Korean
- English
- Either is fine

**4. Where are you based?** *(Short answer · required)* — e.g. "Seoul, Korea"

## Section 2 — Your studies

**5. University / school** *(Short answer · required)*

**6. Current year** *(Multiple choice · required)*
- 1st year
- 2nd year
- 3rd year
- 4th year
- Master's
- PhD
- Graduated
- Other

**7. Major / field of study** *(Short answer · required)*

**8. Expected graduation (month / year)** *(Short answer · optional)*

## Section 3 — Your goals

**9. What do you want to get out of this program?** *(Paragraph · required)*

**10. What kind of role are you aiming for in the U.S.?** *(Checkboxes · required — pick all that apply)*
- Software engineering
- Data / ML / AI
- Research
- Product / PM
- Founding / startup
- Still exploring
- Other

**11. Target timeline for working in the U.S.** *(Multiple choice · optional)*
- Within 1 year
- 1–2 years
- 2+ years
- Not sure yet

**12. What's your biggest question or challenge right now?** *(Paragraph · optional)* — e.g. visas, recruiting, interviews, choosing companies

## Section 4 — Background & links

**13. LinkedIn profile** *(Short answer · optional)*

**14. GitHub / portfolio** *(Short answer · optional)*

**15. Résumé / CV** *(File upload · optional)* — accept PDF; this requires signing in to Google to upload.

**16. Relevant experience** *(Paragraph · optional)* — internships, projects, research, etc.

---

## Section 5 — Logistics & consent


**18. How did you hear about Alonzo?** *(Short answer · optional)*

**19. Anything else you'd like us to know?** *(Paragraph · optional)*

**20. Consent** *(Checkboxes · required)*
- ☐ I agree to be contacted about my application and matched with a mentor.

## After you publish
1. Click **Send → 🔗 link → Copy** to get the `https://forms.gle/...` URL.
2. Paste that URL into `index.html` everywhere it says `REPLACE_WITH_YOUR_GOOGLE_FORM` (the nav "Apply" button and the hero button).
3. Submit a test response yourself to confirm it lands in your responses sheet.
